How to Play

The Premise

You are Detective Voss, invited to a winter gala at Ravenholm Manor. At midnight, Lord Ashworth is found murdered. Then you wake up... and it's the same morning again. You're trapped in a time loop.

Your Goal

Use each loop to gather evidence, interrogate suspects, eavesdrop on conversations, and piece together the truth. Your knowledge persists across loops — the day resets, but you remember everything.

Time Management

Each action costs time. Moving between rooms takes 15 minutes. Conversations take 10-20 minutes. You have from 6:00 AM to midnight — use your time wisely. You can wait or fast-forward to catch specific events.

Investigation

Examine objects to find clues. Talk to NPCs — new dialogue options unlock as you discover evidence. Eavesdrop on conversations by being in the right place at the right time.

The Notebook

Press N to open your notebook. Track clues, NPC profiles, the timeline, and build your evidence board by connecting clues. When ready, make your accusation.
